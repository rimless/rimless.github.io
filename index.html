Pengda Xiang

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC), working on Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. I'm working with Prof. Yajie Zhao, Prof. Xuezhe Ma and Prof. Hao Li. I finished my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at IIIS (Yao Class, the Special Pilot CS Class led by Andrew Chi-Chih Yao), Tsinghua University.

My research interest currently lies in Computer Vision (2D & 3D), especially Generative Models.
